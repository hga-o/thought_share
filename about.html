This project was an attempt to provide a safe means of surfacing thoughts and feelings. In Part 1, I created 10 individual booklets of a contained thought, which were then shared in a classroom of 12 people. In Part 2, the readers created their own booklet of contained thoughts, using the medium as a vehicle to project their thoughts annonymously. Everyone's (and my) creations are now documented on this webpage as an archive. Feel free to click and view.

Project by Helen Gao for Materiality of Selfhood. Instructed by
Codi Filardi and Rosemary Engstrom. RISD Wintersession 2018.
